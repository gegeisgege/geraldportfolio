Research

Jurnal Teknik ITS 2025

Hierarchical Cluster Analysis of Medical Student Performance Across Multiple Assessment Modalities

Gerald Mahapranaja Pillian & Ahmad Muklason, S.Kom., M.Sc., Ph.D.

Identified three performance clusters — At-Risk (26.5%), Mixed Performance (9.2%), Average (64.3%) — from 98 medical students across written, practical, standardised patient, and problem-based assessments. Validated via one-way ANOVA (p < 0.001), Silhouette, Calinski-Harabasz, and Davies-Bouldin indices.
